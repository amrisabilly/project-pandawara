About Us

Pandawara is more than just an environmental group we are a community of individuals driven by a shared commitment to environmental stewardship. Our diverse team comprises environmentalists, activists, and volunteers, all working together towards a common goal.

Our Vision

Healthy environment and caring society

Our Mission

To achieve this vision, we embark on a multifaceted approach. Through grassroots initiatives and collaborative partnerships, we clean rivers, restore ecosystems, and respond swiftly to environmental emergencies.
